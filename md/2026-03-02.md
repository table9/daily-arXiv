### Diode Effect May Assist Finding Proper Superconductivity Mechanism in Copper Oxides
- **arXiv**: [2602.23552](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23552)  ·  [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.23552.pdf)
- **Title (EN)**: Diode Effect May Assist Finding Proper Superconductivity Mechanism in Copper Oxides
- **Authors**: Armen Gulian, Serafim Teknowijoyo, Vahan Nikoghosyan
- **Categories**: cond-mat.supr-con
- **TL;DR**: 铜氧化物高温超导体在零磁场下表现出时间反演对称性破缺，通过Tl₂Ba₂CaCu₂O₈微桥在100K观测到超导二极管效应，该非互易响应在±100Oe磁场下保持不变。

We present measurements demonstrating that copper-oxide high-temperature superconductors can exhibit broken time-reversal symmetry in the absence of external magnetic fields. Using $Tl_{2}Ba_{2}CaCu_{2}O_{8}$ microbridges, we observe a pronounced superconducting diode effect at 100 K under strictly zero-field conditions. Our results are consistent with recent reports of zero-field diode behavior in $Bi_{2}Sr_{2}CaCu_{2}O_{8+δ }$ and together indicate that time-reversal symmetry breaking may be an intrinsic property of the cuprate superconducting state. These findings significantly constrain theoretical models of high-temperature superconductivity that rely on time-reversal-symmetric mechanisms.

- **arXiv**: [2602.23680](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23680)  ·  [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.23680.pdf)
- **Title (EN)**: Intrinsic translational symmetry-breaking charge stripes in underdoped iron pnictides
- **Authors**: Qiang-Jun Cheng, Cong-Cong Lou, Yong-Wei Wang, Ze-Xian Deng, Xu-Cun Ma, Qi-Kun Xue, Can-Li Song
- **Categories**: cond-mat.supr-con, cond-mat.mtrl-sci, cond-mat.str-el
- **TL;DR**: 在铁基超导体中首次明确观测到单向电荷条纹序，作为介于向列相和超导相之间的中间电子相，揭示了从向列性到超导的相干路径

Despite being well established in cuprates, an intrinsic translational symmetry-breaking charge order has not been clearly identified in iron-based superconductors. Using spectroscopic-imaging scanning tunneling microscopy on epitaxial Ca(Fe1-xCox)2As2 (x = 0 ~ 0.055) thin films, we observe smectic, near-commensurate charge-stripe order in the underdoped regime that intervenes between the nematic parent phase and optimally doped superconductivity. Our findings highlight charge ordering as a unifying element across different families of high-temperature superconductors.

### Tensor Hypercontraction Error Correction Using Regression
- **arXiv**: [2602.23567](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23567)  ·  [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.23567.pdf)

- **Authors**: Ishna Satyarth, Eric C. Larson, Devin A. Matthews
- **Categories**: physics.chem-ph, cs.LG
- **TL;DR**: 使用机器学习校正张量超收缩(THC)近似在MP3计算中的误差，通过训练回归模型将THC-MP3误差降低6-9倍

Overall, RBSOG provides a practical and scalable route to reduce time-to-solution and communication cost in large-scale NPT simulations.
